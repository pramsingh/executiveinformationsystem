Executive Information System (EIS) provides a series of customizable, easy to use, reliable, secure and scalable dashboards for vulnerability and security management.

EIS provides continuous real time monitoring of products, devices and systems as well as high-level visualizations of statistics. Centered on Business Continuity, Disaster Prevention, Incident Response Team Evaluations, and IT System Risk Analysis (Hardware & Software), EIS provides a one stop shop where risk perception is made in real-time to enhance enterprise-wide visibility, collaboration and decision-making.
